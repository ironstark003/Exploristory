Prof. Jinu Koshi

Heritage Learning & Pedagogy Mentor

Dr.Jinu Koshy is currently working as the Excavation In-charge at Department of Ancient History and Archaeology, University of Madras. He has lead over 15 archaeological excavations in and around Tamilnadu, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh. He specializes in Palaeolithic Archaeology, Lithic Analysis, Pottery Analysis, Rock Art, Geoarchaeology and Field Archaeology. He has over 25 peer reviewed articles in scientific journals. He is presently working on the Settlement Pattern of ancestors from Lower Palar River Basin and is working on Rock art sites from Kurnool District.
